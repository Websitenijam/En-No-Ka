OUR STORY

縁な子

Discover Our Culinary Journey

Founded in 2023, En Na Ko (meaning "fateful encounter") brings authentic Japanese culinary traditions to Manila. Our master chef, trained in Kyoto's finest kitchens, crafts each dish with precision and respect for seasonality.

We source only the freshest ingredients, many imported directly from Japan, to create an unforgettable dining experience that balances tradition with modern techniques. Our name reflects our belief that every meal should be a meaningful encounter.
